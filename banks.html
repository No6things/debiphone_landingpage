Banks

Any bank can affiliate with Debiphone to provide its customers
a new safer and additional payment channel

  • Allow your customers to have the option to use an innovative, less costly and much safer payment channel
  • Increase your customer base by incorporating Debiphone Virtual Clients who have received payments from your own customers
  • Reduce the cost of maintaining expensive and cumbersome ATM’s
  • Reduce the number of fraud claims
  • Reduce your retail transactions costs with expensive clearinghouses

Virtual Holding Account

  • The Users Virtual Holding Account is only a System’s Account where a Bank Customer blocks off a specific amount to be dealt only electronically through Debiphone
  • Virtual Holding Accounts act as the digital image of the Bank´s own Cash Accounts created solely for the purpose of dealing with Debiphone transactions
  • Every new Debiphone User who creates an account and registers himself/herself with the Application will automatically be created a new Virtual Holding Account
  • Anybody can have a Virtual Holding Account, not only the Bank’s Customers
  • Virtual Holding Accounts can send and receive money to and from other users through Debiphone at any time without a Bank Account.
  • For Virtual Holding Account non-bank Users to withdraw its money on balance they will need to formalize a regular bank account with any of the affiliated Banks

Cashing Out

  • Funds are available for withdrawal from their accounts at the end of the Compensation period.
  • Funds will be made available at the User’s Bank Cash Account

Compensation

  • The Clearinghouse process is a simple process of compensating every transaction and balancing out the individual Virtual Holding Accounts. This process is done directly by Debiphone platform without the need of third party intervention, for higher security purposes
  • The Bank’s Cash Accounts are updated by the Banks following the Clearinghouse process
  • Bank compensation is done several times a day at randomly selected times
  • Compensation does not occur at the time of a given transaction between two users
  • At the closing of any given compensation period, new deposits and withdrawals into anyone of the user’s Virtual Holding Accounts are being reckoned

Compensation Schedule

  • Period 1: Monday – Friday at or around 8 AM.
  • Period 2: Monday – Friday at or around 12 PM.
  • Period 3: Monday – Friday at or around 4 PM.
  • Period 4: Monday – Friday at or around 8 PM.
