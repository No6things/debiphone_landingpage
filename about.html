What is Debiphone?

  • It’s an ePayment Mobile Application that allows users to transfer money safely to any other Smartphone holder with or without the Application installed in their handheld
  • It’s a free application aimed at reducing the use of cash money for the payment of goods and services without the limitation of verifying proof of funds with Banks and Credit Cards issuers. This is part of the “Offline” nature of the Application.

Benefits

For Clients

  • Cash-Free transactions
  • Money is never lost, even though the device is lost, broken or stolen
  • Highly secured environment
  • Easy to track down every transaction. All transactions are saved and personalized
  • Better control of money to be spent
  • Money is always available by other traditional ways (ie. wire transfer, checks, credit cards, etc.)
  • No POS charges at affiliated businesses, as Credit and Debit cards do

For Business

  • No cost POS
  • Secured transactions
  • Faster credits to your account
  • Low Implementation cost on Smartphone
  • Fast processing allows for lines to move quicker

For Banks

  • First in and kick-off period exclusivity
  • New clients stream
  • New business opportunities
  • It’s aimed at substituting cash use, not debit or credit cards
  • Huge growth potential
  • Offline system. No validations against Bank servers required
  • Clients Balance is updated several times per day
  • Highly secured. Transaction Sequence may be tracked down

Why Debiphone?

  • No more cash exchange
  • It’s a Mobile application
  • It’s almost free
  • Highly secured, stand-alone application
  • Users limit the balance they want to keep at all times
  • No need to have the App pre-installed on the device to receive money
  • Can be used on any Smartphone
  • It’s based on the System Balances stored on the Application Server, not on the actual available funds to be checked against bank accounts or credit cards
