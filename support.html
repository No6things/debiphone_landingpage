We are here to help.
The following section shall clarify many of your concerns.
Likewise, you are very welcome to contact us.

  • Q1: What kind of devices can use the Application?
  • Any Smartphone, or 3G phone generation or higher.
  • Q2: Can I receive funds on my Mobile device even if I do not have the Application installed in it?
  • Yes. The User sending the funds selects the option to send payment with or without the Application front-end invitation.
  • Q3: How much does it cost to download and use the Application?
  • Nothing. It’s free.
  • Q4: Are there any fees charged for transferring funds to other users?
  • Debiphone will charge a small 0.5% (zero point five percent) transaction fee to the Sender.
  • Q5: Are there any fees charged for receiving funds from other users?
  • No. It’s completely free unless Sender and Receiver have previously agreed to include a fee for service provided by the Charging party.
  • Q6: Are there any fees for the Application’s front-end invitation to other users along with a payment?
  • There are no fees for downloading the Application.
  • Q7: Do I need an account with a Partner Bank to use the Application?
  • No, unless and until you want to cash out funds received. When you receive funds from a User, a Virtual Holding Account (VHA) is created within Debiphone Platform. You will then need to complete a Bank Application for a Cash Account directly with your selected Bank to validate the account and be able to cash those funds out if you ever need it
  • Q8: Do I need to activate account with Partner Bank to transfer funds received in Virtual Holding Account (VHA)?
  • No. You can transfer funds received to other users from your VHA
  • Q9: How long can I keep VHA before activating it with an affiliated Bank?
  • You only need to activate account if you intend to move funds in it to other Bank or to other accounts at the Partner Bank.
  • Q10: Where do I cash the funds?
  • If you have not activated the VHA, you can not cash the funds held in your VHA. You can only cash funds at any Partner Bank’s once you have activated your account. If you are already a client of the Bank, you can cash it at the Partner Bank branch, or through the ATM network, or through any other channel the Bank has made available to you with any of your regular accounts.
  • Q11: Is the VHA closed if I withdraw all funds in it?
  • No.
  • Q12: Can I only activate VHA through one of the Affiliated Banks?
  • Yes.
  • Q13: How much funds can I transfer through the Application?
  • You setup the limits yourself on your Front-End Configuration Menu when you setup the application in your device
  • Q14: Do I lose my money stored in the App if I lose the Mobile device?
  • Absolutely Not. You will always have available all the funds stored in your VHA at the time of the last Update minus any funds you have received in the last 48 working hours which have yet to be updated in your account
  • Q15: Can other people use the App stored in my Mobile?
  • Only if you have authorized him/her. If they find the device or steal it, they will still need your device’s access code and the App Username and Password.
  • Q16: How often is the Balance updated?
  • After every transaction
  • Q17: How do I load funds into the App?
  • From your accounts (ie. Checking, savings, credit cards) at the Partner Bank or even from funds received from other Debiphone Users
  • Q18: If I’m not a customer at the Partner Bank how do I load funds into the App?
  • You need to open a Virtual Holding Account with Debiphone and then linked up with the Partner Bank to open a Cash Account with them. You can then transfer funds into your Cash Accounts through the channels of your own Bank’s regular accounts
  • Q19: Where can I download the App’s Front-End from?
  • Apple iStore or Google Play
  • Q20: If I change devices or lose my device how do I recover my VHA data?
  • Your username will update the new App on your new device with your current setup and balance
